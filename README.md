# Uber-Analytics-Dashboard-Power-BI-project-
This project analyzes Uber ride booking data to provide actionable insights for business decision-making. The dashboard helps stakeholders monitor operations, understand customer behavior, and improve revenue performance.

What We Did on this project:

**Data Cleaning & Preparation**

Removed duplicates, handled missing values, standardized cancellation reasons.

Converted date fields for trend and time-series analysis.

Data Modeling

Built relationships between bookings, drivers, customers, and payment details.

Created calculated columns and DAX measures for KPIs (e.g., revenue, cancellation rate).

**Analysis & KPIs**

Total bookings, completed rides, cancellations, and cancellation rate.

Revenue analysis by booking status, payment method, and vehicle type.

Cancellation insights split by driver vs customer reasons.

Customer & driver rating trends for service quality.

**Visualizations**

KPI Cards → Revenue, Bookings, Cancellations, Avg Booking Value.

Trend Analysis → Monthly/Quarterly rides & revenue growth.

Cancellations Dashboard → Reasons & impact on performance.

Vehicle Analysis → Revenue & distance by vehicle type.

Payment Breakdown → Cash, UPI, Wallet, Card distribution.

**Business Value**

This dashboard enables Uber’s management to:

Track cancellations and identify operational bottlenecks.

Optimize vehicle performance and revenue by type.

Understand customer and driver experience through ratings.

Improve payment strategies by monitoring usage trends.

Take data-driven decisions to enhance customer satisfaction and reduce lost revenue.
